Overview

Purpose

Have you ever had a lot of dirt in your patio that even when you scrub hard it doesn’t come off or do you ever have too much junk in your house and you don’t know where to throw it away? Well that is what we do in Moore’s Construction. We pressure wash to clean the dirt away, and we do junk removal to lessen your stress on how to throw away small or huge garbage. We believe that a clean house gives the brightest people.

Audience

Our target customers are homeowners, who want to ensure cleanliness of their homes as well as people who needs to declutter waste things to gain more space. We also target real estate agents and business man or woman because they can help in expanding the network of our business, since they know an enormous amount of people who owns a house, apartment, restaurants, and other establishments. We can pressure wash there walls, or throw their wastes away.

THE ORIGIN OF MOORE'S CONSTRUCTION

The Moore’s Construction was established on 13th of August 2021. It all started with an idea from Daniel Moore, the founder of Moore’s Construction, he was analytically searching for the exact purpose of building a business that would work all year round, and a business that has a high demand but yet less supply or services. And that is the start of Moore’s Construction. From that point on to as of this date, Daniel Moore was galvanize to make his Limited Liability Company (LLC) into a success. Together with his wife, Danica Moore, they plan to expand the business exponentially by working together. Danica Moore plays a substantial role to the business as she not only utilizes social media to advertise Moore’s Construction, but she gives assistance to Daniel Moore with the day-to-day operation. The couple are currently servicing an average of more or less two clients a week since the 16th of February 2022. It is a marvelous start for them, but they plot to be consistent, and innovate the Moore’s Construction as time goes by.

WHO IS DANICA AND DANIEL MOORE?

Danica and Daniel Moore are a newly married couple who teamed up together to build Supreme Washers. Daniel is a carpenter by profession. He majored in Business Marketing in BYU- Idaho. His dream and focus is to help people by offering quality service at an affordable price. Danica is a registered nurse who graduated from the University of Santo Tomas in the Philippines. While Daniel provides the service, Danica is in charge of the administrative efforts of the business such as marketing and accounting. The couple supports one another in building this business to achieve their dreams of being financially stable while helping many. They chose junk removal and pressure cleaning because they believe a clean home is a stress free home.

CREATION OF WEBSITE

This website was made to ensure customers, the quality of work, Moore’s Construction LLC does. The Moore’s Construction LLC wants to show our customers our previous jobs so they have an idea on what our standard is working with clients. As a matter of fact, we do our best attempt to clean, even the slightest section of the area. In addition to this, it is for them to learn about us. They can also interact with us through a messaging system and book their appointments if they want our service.

The Moore’s Construction LLC’s site aims to provide the answer to questions in regards to the type of services we are offering, the cost of our services, the mode of payments we are accepting, the location we are currently in and the availability of our services.

FOR THE SITE VISITORS

The Moore’s Construction LLC purpose is not only to clean houses, but to build a tidy community. Our aim is to make other people family, not just our customers. We would want them to be able to communicate with us freely, ask us questions or send their inquiries through the website. The customers can also make appointments and payments when they choose to avail our services.

The information that is necessary in our website includes the origin of the Moore’s Construction on how it was slowly and steadily built to create a service that would help people, the founders or owners of the Moore’s Construction LLC, the services we offer, our scheduling for the services and the duration of the service, the pricing of each service and the available promos that can help save money for customers, as well as proofs of the work we have done in the past for our customer’s peace of mind.

We want them to have an easy access to our services. We also want them to know who we are as a business. Being able to put all the necessary information in our website can build trust and a lasting relationship between us and our customers.
