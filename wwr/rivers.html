Desolation and Gray Canyon, Utah

Desolation and Gray Canyon, Utah, a National Landmark, is a 84-mile trip runs from Sand Wash to Swaseys Rapid. This Desolation is rated a Class II/III and is usually done in 6 days. It boasts outstanding scenery, interesting geologic formations, evidence of prehistoric and historic human activity, and great whitewater opportunities.

Grand Canyon, Arizona

Grand Canyon, Arizona is a grand daddy of all canyons, and all river trips. It is a Class I - X. The Grand Canyon combines world-class whitewater with breathtaking scenery to make one truly unforgettable river experience, it is with whitewater rapids, side canyons and ancient indian ruins accessible only by river.
